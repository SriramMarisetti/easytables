About BMI Calculator

The Body Mass Index (BMI) calculator helps you assess if your weight is within a healthy range based on your height. It’s a quick, simple tool used by healthcare professionals to categorize weight status.

BMI Categories:
Underweight: BMI < 18.5
Normal weight: 18.5 ≤ BMI < 24.9
Overweight: 25 ≤ BMI < 29.9
Obese: BMI ≥ 30
