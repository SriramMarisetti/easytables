GPA Calculator

About This Calculator

This GPA Calculator lets you calculate your Grade Point Average based on your course grades and credit hours. GPA is calculated by dividing the total grade points by total credit hours.

Formula:
GPA = (Sum of (Grade × Credit Hours)) ÷ (Total Credit Hours)

It's perfect for tracking academic performance, goal setting, or checking eligibility for scholarships and honors.
